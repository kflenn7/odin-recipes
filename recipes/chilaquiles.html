Chilaquilles

Description

This is my favorite dish from my Wife, which she makes for us on Sunday mornings.

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas
  • Serrano Pepper
  • Tomato
  • Egg (Optional)

Steps

  1. Make the Salsa (Tomato and Serrano)
  2. Cut the Corn Tortillas
  3. Fry the Corn Tortillas
  4. Mix the Salsa and Tortillas
  5. Cook an optional fried egg
