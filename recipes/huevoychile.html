Huevo y Chile

Description

This is my favorite dish from my Grandmother, and one that I am sure is seen in other forms around the world.

Ingredients

  • Serrano Pepper
  • Tomato
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Cook the Tomato and Serrano
  2. Make the Salsa (Tomato and Serrano)
  3. Scarmble the Eggs
  4. Mix the Salsa into the Eggs
